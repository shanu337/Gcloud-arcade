# 📡 Pub/Sub (Messaging System)

## 🧠 What is Pub/Sub?
A messaging service used for event-driven communication between services.

---

## 🔑 Key Concepts

### 📤 Publisher
Sends messages to a topic

### 📥 Subscriber
Receives messages from a subscription

### 🧵 Topic
Channel where messages are sent

### 📦 Subscription
Connection to receive messages

---

- Created topics and subscriptions

## ⚠️ Challenges Faced

## 💡 Key Takeaways
- Enables scalable communication between services
- Useful in real-time systems & microservices
